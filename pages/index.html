About Soft Skill Studio

Founded by Smita Deka

Softskillstudio is an initiative started by Smita Deka amidst the pandemic in May 2020. This initiative has been taken with the motive of empowering the NorthEast youth and bringing them at par with all their global peers.
The difference between ordinary and extraordinary is that little "Extra". We, at Softskillstudio aim to unleash the true potential of individuals by focusing on their holistic growth. Soft skills combined with self-image development helps create enhancing personalities. This in turn helps boost personal as well as professional growth. We provide a wide array of services to help you upskill and grow in your career.
